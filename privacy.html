Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

1. Information We Collect

HookLink is designed to be a frictionless tool. We do not require you to create an account, and we do not collect personal identifying information (PII) to use the core generator. Any content you input into the generator is processed securely via our AI partners (e.g., Groq, Gemini) solely for the purpose of generating your post and is not stored permanently on our servers.

2. How We Use Your Information

The text you provide is strictly used to generate your requested LinkedIn hooks and posts. We may temporarily store your usage counts (rate limits) in your browser's local storage to prevent abuse of the free tool. We do not sell, rent, or share your content with third-party advertisers.

3. Third-Party Services

We use third-party AI providers to power the text generation. By using HookLink, your text inputs are sent to these providers (such as Groq or Google) under their respective privacy and data processing agreements, which prohibit the use of API data to train their base models.
